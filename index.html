Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026  ·  Developer: Savaş EREN

LyricPad Pro is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

LyricPad Pro collects only the minimum data necessary to provide its services:

  • Notes and files — stored locally on your device. We do not access or transmit your personal notes.
  • Google Account information — only if you choose to sign in with Google for Drive backup. We access your email address and Drive storage solely for backup purposes.
  • Purchase information — subscription status managed by Google Play and RevenueCat. We do not store payment details.
  • App settings and preferences — stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To back up your data to Google Drive (only when you enable this feature)
  • To manage your subscription status via Google Play and RevenueCat
  • To process OCR (text recognition) requests on-device

3. Data Storage & Security

Your notes and files are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server unless you explicitly enable Google Drive backup.

When Google Drive backup is enabled, your files are stored in your personal Google Drive account under the LyricPad Pro folder. We do not have access to your Google Drive outside of this folder.

4. Third-Party Services

LyricPad Pro uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In & Google Drive API — for optional cloud backup. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Lyrics.ovh API — for lyrics search (song title and artist name are sent to search). No personal data is transmitted.
  • Google ML Kit — OCR processing is performed on-device. No images are sent to external servers.

5. Permissions

LyricPad Pro may request the following permissions:

  • Storage — to read and save your note files
  • Camera — for OCR text scanning (optional)
  • Microphone — for the guitar tuner feature (optional)
  • Internet — for Google Drive sync and lyrics search

All permissions are optional except storage. You can deny camera and microphone permissions and still use most features.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data remains on your device and in your Google Drive until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To remove Google Drive backups, delete the LyricPad Pro folder from your Google Drive.

To delete your RevenueCat subscription data, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

LyricPad Pro is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
